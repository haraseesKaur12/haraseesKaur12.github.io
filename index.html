Research profile / 2026

At scale, performance becomes a question of communication.

I study high-performance and distributed computing systems, with particular attention to the communication costs that emerge when many machines must act as one.

My present work examines MPI collective communication, synchronization, trace-driven modeling, and scalability-aware design for large systems and distributed AI workloads.
